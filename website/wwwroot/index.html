Welcome

This is my own little website/tinkering space. Nothing special to see on here quite yet but I'm gonna continue updating this as I go (might be a good idea to look at this as kinda my own little blog I suppose).

Steampunk

One of my favorite topics to talk about is Steampunk. It's a genre, scene, style and a lifestyle. Steampunk usually plays in a victorian fantasy scenario.

Cyberpunk

Yet another of my favorite topics, following the same vein as Steampunk. The difference here is that Cyberpunk plays in a dark dystopian future (as thought of from an 80s perspective). Big works that influenced me here were most of William Gibson's books, as well as the pen-and-paper Shadowrun.

Programming

Kinda plays into the Cyberpunk that I like so much, I like programming while listening to some chill beats.

Music

Metal, Cabaret, some Pop, some Soundtracks (Made in Abyss by Kevin Penkin) as well as most things electronic I'm constantly listening to. There's only a few things that you will never hear me listen to, german Schlager, gangster Rap, etc.

Made in Abyss

I'm a bit of a weeb so I like and have watched plenty of Manga and Anime. My absolute favorite out of the literal over 100 is by far Made in Abyss. It is a tragic and beautiful journey filled with blessings and curses.

Lord of the Rings

Having first read the books as a child and then later watched all of the movies, I can say that Tolkien's worldbuilding is inspiring and fascinating. I've also read a lot of his lesser known works, such as the Silmarillion (that was a big pain to sit through!).
